INNOVATION


Decentralized Exchanges

DEXs are a significant innovation in the crypto exchange space. Unlike traditional centralized exchanges (CEXs), DEXs operate on blockchain technology and allow users to trade cryptocurrencies directly with one another without the need for an intermediary.

Crypto Derivatives Trading

Many crypto exchanges have introduced derivatives trading, enabling users to speculate on the price of cryptocurrencies without actually owning them. These derivatives include futures, options, and perpetual contracts.
